I went with “Z” because of it being the first letter of my last name and i played with the fonts a bit and decided to just go with cursive. It was simple yet I felt as though it would leave a good fun design for the final form. I didn’t want to make the letters too big , but they are smallest to biggest , at first I played with the biggest being in the middle and bouncing off that to create the drawing but liked how the smallest looked at the center. I sketched it out first and thought about having them all connected closely but it didn’t suit my likely so to keep the rows neat yet shaped I went with connecting them by the ends of each other and moving the letter around. I went with 4 rows , due to the limited space.
