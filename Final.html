For this assignment I went with showing a current issue that is widely well known. What is happening in Palestinian and it’s people. The article chosen speaks about the fact that these families are being driven out of their homes due to the bombing and it’s also causing them to lose said houses. I wanted to do cut outs and use pictures that present what’s going on not specifically of the fact that the houses are being destroyed but as well as how it’s affecting everyone and the country itself. I went with pictures showing the unfortunate after math of the bombings and with pictures representing Palestinian. I went with a black border as well to lock in the elements from within as well on of the the pictures I used wording from the article.
